# ISO/IEC AI Standards (25058, 5259, 42001)

**Source:** [iso.org](https://iso.org)

**Accessed:** 2026-01-31

## ISO/IEC 25058:2024 (Quality)

- **Functional Correctness:** Does the AI output meet specs?
- **Performance Efficiency:** Resource usage.
- **Reliability:** Stability over time.

## ISO/IEC 5259-2:2024 (Data Quality)

- **Accuracy, Completeness, Consistency.**
- AI datasets often suffer from "model collapse" or synthetic loops.

## ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (Management)

- **Governance:** Risk management and transparency.
- AI systems require auditable trails (feature: "Trustworthiness").
