# Expected Artifact Notes

- Treat the preprint and version-of-record as related but distinct records.
- Keep the preprint canonical entry separate from the journal article unless the
  active policy explicitly collapses them.
- Record a version relationship in verification metadata so the later article can
  be linked without overwriting the preprint.
- Flag the version-of-record note for review because the intake includes both a
  preprint DOI and a journal DOI.
- Do not silently replace the preprint citation with the journal citation.
