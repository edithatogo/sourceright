# Expected Artifact Notes

- Keep the original CSL-like bibliographic entry unchanged unless a separate
  canonical correction is explicitly approved.
- Add a verification-sidecar note that the work is retracted and that the PDF
  header presents a corrected-copy signal.
- Surface a review item for the retraction and version warning rather than
  silently merging the replacement PDF into the canonical record.
- Preserve the original DOI relationship and attach the retraction notice as
  evidence metadata.
- Do not rewrite the reference into a different article or infer a new edition.
