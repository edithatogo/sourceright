# Track 90 - Open Platform and Preprint Coverage

## Goal

Extend Sourceright's platform roadmap beyond OJS, Janeway, enterprise adapters, and arXiv into a comprehensive coverage map for open journal, manuscript, preprint, overlay, repository, and peer-review platforms.

## User outcome

A maintainer can see which open and service-only platforms are supported, contracted, being researched, or out of scope, including preprint and review systems.

## Scope

- Track OJS, Janeway, Kotahi, PubPub, OpenReview, HotCRP, OpenConf, PKP Open Preprint Systems, arXiv, bioRxiv, medRxiv, OSF Preprints, Zenodo, InvenioRDM, DSpace, EPrints, Episciences, PREreview, Review Commons, PubPeer, SciPost, Qeios, and F1000Research-style models.
- Classify each platform as open source, public service, proprietary, repository, review service, overlay platform, or model reference.
- Define reconnaissance gates before adapter claims.
- Define adapter contract and fixture requirements for each target.
- Preserve separate labels for implemented, contracted, reconnaissance required, service-only, and out-of-scope states.

## Out of scope

- Claiming support for all platforms.
- Integrating with proprietary or public services without API, terms, privacy, and credential review.
- Uploading manuscripts to external services without explicit policy and approval.

## Completion criteria

- Platform matrix is documented.
- Missing coverage is explicit.
- Open preprint and open review lanes are created.
- Adapter claim gates are recorded.
