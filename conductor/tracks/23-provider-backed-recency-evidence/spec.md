# Provider-Backed Recency Evidence Spec

## Goal

Wire retraction, correction, preprint, superseded-guideline, and publication-age evidence into Sourceright policy and reporting.

## Scope

- Capture provider-backed integrity and recency evidence.
- Surface retraction, correction, expression-of-concern, preprint, guideline supersession, and publication-age signals.
- Feed evidence into reference reporting and journal screening outputs.
- Preserve the distinction between evidence signals and claim truth.
- Avoid assertions of AI authorship.

## Outputs

- Recency and integrity evidence records in verification sidecars.
- Policy classification for report severity.
- Reference integrity report fields.
- Journal screening report fields.
- Documentation for evidence interpretation.

## Boundaries

This track surfaces provider-backed evidence. It must not assert whether a claim is true, whether a citation proves a claim, or whether a manuscript was written by AI.

Canonical CSL remains clean bibliographic data. Recency and integrity signals belong in sidecar evidence and reporting outputs.
