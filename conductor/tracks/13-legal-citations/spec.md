# Legal Citations Spec

## Goal

Plan legal citation verification without forcing legal citation data into academic CSL semantics.

## Scope

- Cases, legislation, regulations, neutral citations, reporters, courts, jurisdictions, dates, pinpoints, and subsequent treatment.
- Public legal citation APIs and open legal data sources.
- Legal manual-review workflow with stronger jurisdictional provenance.

## Boundaries

This is a future track. Academic reference verification remains the first implementation priority.
