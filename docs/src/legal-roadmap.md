# Legal Citation Roadmap

Legal citations are a future track. They should not be forced into academic CSL semantics where the data model does not fit.

The legal model needs to represent:

- Cases.
- Legislation and regulations.
- Neutral citations.
- Reporters.
- Courts and jurisdictions.
- Pinpoint references.
- Subsequent treatment.

Candidate provider research includes public legal citation APIs such as CourtListener and Caselaw Access Project.
